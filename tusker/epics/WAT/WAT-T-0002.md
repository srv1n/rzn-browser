## Intent

ChatGPT shipped a controlled radix RadioGroup for the model/effort picker. Synthetic clicks (`dispatchEvent`, fiber `onClick`/`onSelect`, parent `onValueChange`, keyboard fallbacks) close the menu but never flip `aria-checked` — the silent fallback that downstream callers were hitting (asked for Pro Extended, got Pro Standard). Fix: drive the radio commits through CDP `Input.dispatchMouseEvent` via `click_element` `use_cdp:true` (already wired in the extension), and verify the commit by reopening the menu and parsing `<Model>• <Effort>` on the checked top-level testid. Hard-fail on mismatch so downstream cannot send under the wrong model.
